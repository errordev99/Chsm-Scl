About School

Chowgaccha Hazi Sardar Mortoz Ali Secondary School

Chowgaccha Hazi Sardar Mortoz Ali Secondary School, located in Chowgachha Upazila, Jessore District, Bangladesh, is dedicated to delivering quality education at the secondary level. Following the national curriculum, the school emphasizes academic excellence and personal growth. It plays a significant role in shaping the educational landscape of the area, offering a range of subjects to help students succeed in national examinations. The institution strives to create a disciplined and enriching learning environment, serving as a key educational establishment in its community.
